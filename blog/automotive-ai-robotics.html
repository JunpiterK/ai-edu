Automotive AI & Robotics

Vehicle factories need a different AI playbook than fabs.

Fab AI thinks in chambers, recipes, wafers, traces, and yield. Automotive manufacturing AI thinks in VINs, stations, takt time, robot cells, torque records, vision images, Andon events, and safety zones.

Field boundary

I keep this series separate because automotive plants punish lazy analogies. A robot cell, takt window, Andon event, and VIN history create a different rhythm from wafers and chambers. The overlap with FAB AI is useful, but the boundary matters.
