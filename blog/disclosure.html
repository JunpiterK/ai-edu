Ads, affiliate links, and editorial independence.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Advertising

AI Edu Archive may display ads through Google AdSense or similar advertising networks. Ads help keep the archive free to read. We try to place ads away from code examples, navigation, and core reading flow.

Affiliate Links

Some articles may contain affiliate links. If you buy through those links, AI Edu Archive may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Product Recommendations

Recommendations are based on relevance to the article: AI, robotics, on-prem LLMs, RAG, agents, semiconductor AI, and engineering education. A higher commission is not a reason to recommend a product.

Editorial Independence

Sponsored content, if ever published, will be clearly labeled. Technical conclusions, architecture choices, and warnings are written for reader usefulness, not advertiser preference.

Reader-first rule. Every monetized page should still stand on its own as a useful free article. If a product link weakens trust, it does not belong in the article.
