Knowledge you cannot search

Ask a new engineer "what's the response SOP for chamber alarm E-1420?" and the honest answer is often "ask the senior on B-shift." That knowledge is real and documented — and unsearchable. Keyword search over a thousand PDFs returns either nothing or everything.

RAG turns documents into a semantic index: you ask in natural language, the system retrieves the passages that actually answer you, and a local LLM writes the response with citations. 근거 문서를 인용하므로 환각을 걸러낼 수 있습니다.

RAG isn't a chatbot bolted onto your docs. It's a search engine that reads the results for you — and shows its work.

Why chunk size decides everything

Move the sliders and watch a real SOP split into coloured chunks in real time. Too small and a chunk loses its context; too large and one chunk blurs several topics. 만지며 체감하세요.

Rule of thumb. 800–1,200 characters is a solid default for procedural docs, with ~15% overlap. Dense SOPs want smaller chunks; narrative deviation reports want larger. Measure hit-rate on real questions before you commit.

The numbers behind the knob

Chunk size vs retrieval quality

Two forces pull opposite ways: tiny chunks lose context, huge chunks blur topics. Retrieval quality peaks in the middle. Conceptual curves, honestly labelled. 개념적 곡선.

Figure 5. Chunk size is a trade-off, not a constant. The coral curve — what you actually care about — peaks where chunks are big enough to be answerable but small enough to stay on one topic.

Tips that actually matter

  • Chunk size is a trade-off. Dense SOPs want smaller chunks; narrative reports want larger. Measure hit-rate on real questions first.
  • Metadata filters cut noise. Tag by equipment and process, filter at query time. An etcher question should never retrieve a CVD manual.
  • Always surface sources. A cited answer is verifiable. Without citations you've built a confident guesser; with them, a search tool. 출처 표기가 환각 방지의 핵심입니다.
  • Match embedder to language. Korean docs need bge-m3 or multilingual-e5. An English-only model quietly wrecks recall on 한국어.
  • Nothing leaves the network. Loaders, embeddings, vector DB, and LLM all run local. 데이터 무반출.

That's a complete air-gapped RAG stack in five short files. Start with one document folder and a dozen real questions, tune chunk_size and k against them, and grow from there.
